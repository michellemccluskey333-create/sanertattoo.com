About Me

Artist-led, client-centered tattooing.

I’m a New York native and have been tattooing professionally since 2010.

I’m drawn to bold imagery with real movement, rooted in traditional Western and Japanese styles.

My work is influenced by the raw power of artists like Frank Frazetta, as well as tattooers like Ed Hardy, Brian Bruno, Danielle Hoang, and Tim Lehi.

I aim to translate any idea into a tattoo that feels alive, hits hard, and holds up over time.
